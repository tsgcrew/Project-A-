## Company Overview

'A' is a mid-sized Canadian retail company operating 75 physical stores across urban and suburban markets. The company specializes in lifestyle and apparel products targeting mid-income consumers.

### Current Challenges
- 35% decline in in-store foot traffic post-COVID
- Increasing competition from e-commerce platforms
- Legacy systems limiting operational efficiency
- Limited digital capabilities across the workforce

### Transformation Goals
- Launch a fully integrated e-commerce platform
- Implement AI-driven inventory management
- Transition to omnichannel retail model
- Upskill workforce for digital operations

### People Impact
- Role redesign for store employees
- Increased demand for digital skills
- Risk of resistance due to job uncertainty
