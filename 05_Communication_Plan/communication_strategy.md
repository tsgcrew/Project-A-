## Communication Strategy

| Audience | Message | Channel | Frequency |
|----------|--------|--------|----------|
| All Employees | Why transformation matters | Townhall | Monthly |
| Managers | How to lead teams through change | Workshops | Bi-weekly |
| Executives | Progress and risks | Reports | Monthly |

### Communication Principles
- Transparency
- Consistency
- Two-way feedback

### Key Insight
Lack of communication is the biggest driver of resistance—not the change itself.
