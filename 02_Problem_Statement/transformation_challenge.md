## Transformation Challenge

'A'’s transformation introduces significant organizational and behavioral change.

### Key Risks
- Employee resistance to new technologies
- Skill gaps in digital tools
- Misalignment between leadership and frontline staff
- Decreased morale due to perceived job insecurity

### Core Question
How can 'A' implement its digital strategy while ensuring employees are engaged, capable, and willing to adopt new ways of working?

### Strategic Hypothesis
A structured change management approach combining leadership alignment, targeted communication, and capability building will significantly improve adoption and reduce resistance.
