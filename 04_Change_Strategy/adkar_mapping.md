## ADKAR Change Strategy

### Awareness
- Executive-led town halls explaining business urgency
- Clear articulation of “why change is needed”

### Desire
- Incentives tied to adoption metrics
- Highlight career growth opportunities

### Knowledge
- Structured training programs on new systems
- Role-specific learning paths

### Ability
- Hands-on workshops and pilot programs
- On-the-job support and coaching

### Reinforcement
- Performance metrics linked to adoption
- Recognition programs for early adopters
